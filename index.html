The Secret Show was slated to be one of adult swims classic core programing back in 2005 but due to budgetary issues and a general lack of public interest in the show caused it to have one and only airing on April 20th, 2005. The tape of the only airing was then filed deep within storage at adult swims Georgia HQ until now. Our team has found the footage, rendered it from tape on to this new digital medium and are now broadcasting it freely for the public at large to enjoy. If you enjoyed the show please tweet at adult swim #bringbackthesecretshow so they may hopefully fund more episodes in the future.
